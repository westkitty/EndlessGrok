# Starsilk Lore Adaptation

## Source Basis Labels

- `direct canon`: named or explicitly defined by the Starsilk source package.
- `canon-faithful adaptation`: derived from source anchors without claiming direct text reuse.
- `interpretive adaptation`: new game-facing content that follows canon locks.
- `mechanical UI necessity`: required for implementation, testing, or interaction clarity.

## Canon Safety Warnings

- Starsilk is not generic space fantasy.
- Do not use spider, web, webbing, spider legs, or spider symbols.
- Drakken are not dragons. Use bio-geologic overwrite and strain-coded catastrophe.
- Syrin Reagent implies containment vapor, bypass, or inerting, not blood or purity.
- Archive Data is record/data trace, not soul currency.
- Blood Ring Glass is atrocity-linked and must not be glamorized.
- Starbinding, heliocide, collapsed stars, and final macro execution must report consequence and cost, not triumph.

## Tooltip Rule

Every gameplay-facing tooltip must present: mechanical effect first, lore second, warning third when applicable.
